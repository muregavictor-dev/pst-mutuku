Who Is Pastor Mutuku

A voice for those done performing faith.

For over fourteen years, Pastor Mutuku has stood between the pulpit and the ordinary lives of the people he serves in Kutus — evangelising, discipling, and mentoring the next generation of believers, while refusing to trade depth for applause. His writing carries the same conviction as his preaching: faith was never meant to be comfortable, and obedience was never meant to be optional.
